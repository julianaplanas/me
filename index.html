Juliana Planas

I have been training in the field of digital technology, learning different programming languages, in order to complement them with my degree in linguistics. My goal is to be a meeting point between web development, language and art. I like to train and learn, so teamwork is my thing. In addition, I am always developing a personal project, learning a new language or starting a different sport. Projects

Events

Sepicall

Co-creator, producer, community management and moderation during the party

2020

Sepicall worked as a virtual community that looked to offer a dissemination platform for diverse artists, DJs and emerging producers who, in the context of preventive and compulsory social distancing, saw their sources of income diminished. In addition, it offered entertainment in a space of enjoyment, dance and socialization for all people, in a context that prevents enjoying the public space to socialize.

Alta Paja

Co-creator, producer, community and artist management

2017-2019

A show whose main focus was contemporary poetry, but which combined other disciplines such as music, scenic and visual with the intention of making artistic sensuality and sexual diversity visible in equal measure.

Todxs lxs chicxs

Creator, producer and community management

2017

Cultural collective composed by a diversity of artists, poets, musicians, illustrators, designers, photographers, actors and all those interested in art and who would like to spread their work. A poetic and performative show was organized at the Centro Cultural Recoleta based on a call, where four plaquettes were also published that included 20 sub25 poets chosen from an open call. In addition, other events, fairs and open microphones were organized in different cultural centers in order to give a space to new and young voices.
